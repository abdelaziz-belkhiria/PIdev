About Our Platform

Comprehensive Care for Post-Kidney Transplant Patients

Our secure, distributed platform centralizes patient records for pediatric nephrology, especially post-kidney transplant care. Built on a microservices architecture, the system enables real-time monitoring, improves care coordination, and supports clinical decision-making through specialized modules designed specifically for young kidney patients and their healthcare teams.
